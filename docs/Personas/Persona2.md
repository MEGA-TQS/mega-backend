# Eva Martins
**Name:** Eva Martins
**Age:** 27
**Occupation:** Marketing Specialist
**Location:**  Lisbon, Portugal

## Background  
Eva lives an active lifestyle but doesn’t own much sports equipment due to limited storage in her apartment and her preference for trying different sports without committing to purchases. She often participates in weekend paddleboarding, hiking trips, and casual cycling with friends.
 

## Daily Life  
- Works 9–5 in a hybrid schedule
 
- Goes to the gym or yoga class 3–4 times a week

- Plans weekend outdoor activities
 
- Follows sports/fitness influencers on Instagram
 
- Uses mobile apps for most of her bookings: rides, groceries, events, etc.


## Goals & Needs  
- Affordable access to high-quality sports equipment
 
- Quick, easy rentals without complicated logistics
 
- Transparent pricing
 
- Reliable reviews and ratings for equipment and owners
 
- Convenient pick-up/drop-off options


## Pain Points & Challenges  
- Buying equipment is too expensive and takes up too much space

- Hard to know if rented equipment will be in good condition

- Doesn’t want to deal with unclear communication or hidden fees

- Last-minute weekend plans make it hard to book in advance
  

## Motivation  
- Exploring new sports and experiences
 
- Staying active without making large purchases
 
- Being spontaneous with weekend adventures
 
- Saving money while accessing quality gear
