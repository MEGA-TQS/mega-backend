# Sara Coutinho
**Name:** Sara Coutinho
**Age:** 31
**Occupation:** Project Consultant
**Location:**  Braga, Portugal

## Background  
Sara frequently organizes weekend trips with her partner and friends. She loves planning everything well in advance—routes, activities, equipment, accommodation. She doesn’t own much sports gear to avoid clutter and maintenance, so she prefers renting equipment weeks early to ensure availability. 

## Daily Life  
- Works full-time with a structured schedule
- Uses a digital calendar for everything
- Compares prices and conditions before booking
- Avoids last-minute stress
- Goes hiking regularly and plans weekend getaways

## Goals & Needs  
- Ability to book equipment for specific dates in advance
- Guaranteed availability and clear confirmation
- Transparent pricing and cancellation policy
- Convenient pick-up or delivery options
- Ability to reserve multiple items for a group


## Pain Points & Challenges  
- Frustrated when equipment isn’t available on key dates
- Dislikes unclear fees (insurance, delays, hidden charges)
- Doesn’t want to deal with unresponsive owners
- Worried that the equipment might not be in good condition upon pick-up 

## Motivation  
- Keeping trips stress-free
- Ensuring everything goes smoothly for the group
- Avoiding last-minute problems
- Having organized and worry-free weekend experiences
