# EPIC 9
## Platform Administration & KPIs

**Objective**:

Enable the administrator to monitor operations, analyze metrics, and ensure the proper functioning of the platform.

**Description**:

The admin dashboard provides essential business and operational metrics, enabling performance tracking and informed decision-making.

**Context / Personas**:

Platform Administrator.

**Included Features**:

1. Dashboard with KPIs:

    - Active users

    - Listed items

    - Bookings per period

    - Transactional volume

2. Aggregated view of reviews and disputes.

3. Technical monitoring (errors, availability, logs).

4. User and listing management tools.
