# EPIC 6
## Advanced Planning & Group Bookings

**Objective**:

Enable advance bookings and multiple items in a single operation, ensuring unified logistics and securing the desired dates.

**Description**:

Users like Sara, who plan trips in advance, need to book multiple pieces of equipment for groups and ensure that all are available during the same period.

**Context / Personas**:

Sara: needs advanced planning and group management.

**Included Features**:

1. Booking multiple items in a single transaction.

2. Combined availability for the whole group.

3. Single payment for the entire booking.
