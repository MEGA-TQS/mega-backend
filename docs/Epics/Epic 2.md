# EPIC 2
## Equipment Management (Owner Dashboard)

**Objective**:

Provide owners with a simple and efficient way to list, update, and manage their equipment, optimizing time and reducing friction in the process of making items available for rent.

**Description**:

Owners like Alberto need a clear and accessible control panel where they can easily register new items, manage availability, prices, rules, and logistical preferences. This epic supports the complete flow of creating and maintaining listings.

**Context / Personas**:

Alberto: wants a simple, fast, and low-maintenance system.

**Included Features**:

1. Create/edit/remove items.

2. Upload photos and detailed description.

3. Set daily prices and extras.

4. Availability calendar with seasonal rules.

5. Set buffers between bookings (e.g., 48h).

6. Activate/deactivate listings.

7. Manage accessories and additional options.
