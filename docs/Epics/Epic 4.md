# EPIC 4
## Payments, Invoices & Deposits

**Objective**:

Ensure secure and transparent transactions between renters and owners, including payments, insurance, deposits, and receipt issuance.

**Description**:

The system must integrate a secure payment method (in sandbox mode), always display the total cost, and allow for receipt issuance. Owners automatically receive payments after a confirmed return.

**Context / Personas**:

Eva: wants clear pricing and no surprises.

Sara: needs receipts and transparent policies.

Alberto: wants protection against damage and guaranteed payments.

**Included Features**:

1. Sandbox payments (Stripe / PayPal Test Mode).

2. Automatic total price calculation.

3. Optional insurance and extras.

4. Deposit calculation and collection.

5. Digital receipts.

6. Automatic transfer to the owner after return validation.
