# EPIC 5
## Reviews, Reputation & Item Condition

**Objective**:

Increase trust between users through reviews, reputation, and condition verification before and after the rental.

**Description**:

After each booking, renters and owners must be able to review each other. The system must capture the equipment condition via photos and checklists, reducing disputes and promoting transparency.

**Context / Personas**:

Alberto: wants to verify the equipment condition and avoid damage.

Eva and João: need to trust the owners and equipment.

**Included Features**:

1. Bidirectional reviews (Owner ↔ Renter).

2. Equipment quality ratings.

3. Condition checklists before and after the booking.

4. Condition photo upload.

5. Review and behavior history.

6. Credibility badges.
