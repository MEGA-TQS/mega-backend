# EPIC 1 
## Equipment Catalog & Smart Discovery

**Objective**:

Enable users to easily find sports equipment suited to their needs through efficient search, advanced filters, and contextual recommendations.

**Description**:

The catalog is the core of the renter experience. It must allow for quick searching by sport type, location, dates, user level, and other relevant criteria. The results must present clear and trustworthy information, facilitating quick and secure decisions.

**Included Features**:

1. Keyword search (e.g., "kayak", "paddle", "bicycle").

2. Filters by sport, location, price, condition, user level.

3. Availability filter by dates.

4. Highlighting of items with "Instant Book".

5. Recommendations for beginner users.

6. Results page with photos, price, ratings, and availability.
